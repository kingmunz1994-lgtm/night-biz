🏅 Tier system
Customers prove their tier in ZK — they show they hold ≥ the threshold without revealing their exact balance. Tiers are unforgeable and privacy-preserving.
🥉
Bronze
≥ 100 tokens
5% discount · Priority support

≥ 2,000 tokens
20% discount · VIP events · Revenue boost 2×
💎
Platinum
≥ 10,000 tokens
Unlimited perks · Private access · Governance voting
How ZK tiers work: When a customer wants to claim a tier perk, they run the proveTierStatus() circuit from NightBizToken.compact. This generates a ZK proof that their balance ≥ the tier threshold — without revealing their exact token count to the business. The business sees only "this customer is Gold tier" — not their balance.
💰 Revenue sharing
Every sale your business records flows 50% to token holders. Distributed per epoch. Claimed privately on Midnight. Holders never reveal how many tokens they hold.
💳
Record a sale
Every sale is logged on-chain. 50% of net revenue flows to the epoch pool.
🔒
Close epoch
Call closeEpoch() to snapshot holder balances and calculate each holder's share.
⚡
Holders claim
Each holder calls claimRevenue() with a ZK proof of their balance. NIGHT arrives instantly.
